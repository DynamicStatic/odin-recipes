Mexican Style Enchilada

This is a Mexican style enchilada. The enchilada is tasty and I prefer to make this for myself when I'm at home. You should try this with corn tortillas. I like to use red enchilada sauce.

  • tortillas
  • enchilada sauce
  • mexican cheese blend
  • onions

Ingredients

  1. bake and shred chicken
  2. finely chop onion
  3. lightly fry tortillas
  4. wrap chicken, cheese, and onion in tortillas
  5. place tortillas in a casserole
  6. pour enchilada sauce over tortillas
  7. pour shredded cheese and onion over tortillas
  8. bake at 375 for 20 minutes
