Potraost

This is a Mississippi style potroast. The wonderful thing about this is how easy it is to make. The prep time can be less than five minutes if you move quickly. The results are declicous. You only need a little patience and a crock pot.

Ingredients

  • chuck roast
  • 1/2 stick of butter
  • 1 packet of ranch mix
  • 1 packet of Au Jus mix
  • 8 peppercinis

Steps

  1. Place thawed pot roast in crock pot
  2. Pour ranch packet over roast
  3. Pour Au Jus packet over roast
  4. Place stick of butter over roast
  5. Set crock pot to low and let cook for 8 hours
  6. Serve
